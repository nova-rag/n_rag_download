    Ultra-Wideband (UWB) Module Arduino Shield

    Ultra-Wideband (UWB) Innovation @ Qorvo

    Learn all about UWB technology & our products

    View All
    		Product BriefRev B – 04/2021

    Key Features

    • Interoperable with Apple U1 & U2 chip
    • Compatible with Arduino Uno Shield form factor
    • Supports UWB channels 5 (6.5 GHz) and 9 (8 GHz)
    • Fully aligned with FiRa™ PHY, MAC and certification development
    • Incorporates the DWM3000 module containing DW3110 UWB IC and ceramic UWB antenna
    • On-board 3V3 DC-DC
    • Jumper allowing selection of the power supply and current consumption measurements
    • All module pins accessible via Arduino connectors

    Qorvo's DWM3000EVB is an Arduino form factor compatible shield designed for the evaluation of the DWM3000 ultra-wideband (UWB) module for use in a scalable real time location system (RTLS).

    This Arduino shield can be used with the customer's choice of microcontroller. The Arduino shield form factor is supported by many MCU vendors including STMicroelectronics, Nordic Semiconductor and more.

    The DWM3000EVB is designed to be compliant to the FiRa™ PHY and MAC specifications enabling interoperability with other FiRa™ compliant devices.

    The DWM3000EVB is interoperable with the Apple U1 & U2 chip.

    DWM3000EVB was formerly known as DWS3000.

    Qorvo offers two development kits : DW3xxx & QM3xxx SDK v1.0.2 and Qorvo_Nearby_Interaction.

    DW3xxx & QM3xxx SDK v1.0.2 (Released November 2024) facilitates the development of products using the DW3110, DW3120, DW3210, DW3220, QM33110W, QM33120W, and DWM3001C module.

    This SDK is compatible with four hardware development kits: QM33120WDK1, DWM3001CDK, DWM3000EVB and Murata Type2AB.

    It contains sample applications, embedded firmwares, documentation, scripts and a GUI for quick evaluation.

    The Qorvo_Nearby_Interaction to easily evaluate and develop new applications based on location, distance and direction relative to a U1 & U2-equipped iPhone. It contains FiRa Compliant libraries, a sample application, mobile applications and related documentations. 

    The Nearby Interaction Compliant Software enables developers to easily evaluate and develop new applications based on location, distance and direction relative to a U1 & U2-equipped iPhone.

    To get started, download:
    Qorvo Nearby Interaction iOS App (available in the Apple Store)
    Qorvo NI Background iOS App (available in the Apple Store)
